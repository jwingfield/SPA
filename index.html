Shed Planners Association

The Shed Planners Association (BFD) hold an Open Mic and Jam Session on the second Thursday of every month.

We try to mix it up and do something a bit different, so every month the house band plays songs with a theme. So far this year we've had Acoustic April and Movie May, and we're looking forward to Juxtaposed June & July.

The house band covers guitars, bass, keys and drums, so if you're a band or even a solo artist we have a backline set up ready for you to plug in and play.
