Shed Planners Association

The Shed Planners Association (BFD) Open Mic and Jam Session takes place on the second Thursday of every month at the New Inn in Eccleshill, Bradford. We start at 8.30, and if you want to play it's best to get there on time so we can make sure we can fit everybody in.

Each month the Shed Planners House Band has a theme, so that we're not playing the same 12 songs every month. The themes coming up are:

  • Juxtaposed June 9th - Electrified Acoustic
  • Juxtaposed July 14th - Unplugged Rock
  • Advert August 11th - Songs From Adverts (like you couldn't work that out)
  • Second Hit September 8th- The Almost Greatest Hits
