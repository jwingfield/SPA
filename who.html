Shed Planners Association

The Shed Planners Association (BFD) are a crack(ed) team of musicians, seeking fame and fortune, or at least a couple of pints and a good night out. After a drunken night in the New Inn and an incident with an acoustic guitar, somebody had the bright idea of actually doing again deliberately. So, after much deliberation we thought what the hell.

Our house band is mainly the drunken souls that had the fate to touch the guitar on that night, and now Chris, John and Jonathan pick up various other instruments as well including drums and keyboards to play whatever we think we can pull off. Pete, our original bassist has now retired and regular jammer Matt has stepped in to fill the gap along with Mike our resident drummist.

As well as the house band we have a number of regulars and it's been great to see several new bands make their first public outing. The night is open to all, so if you fancy having a go get down early. If you're a soloist or part band that's great - you're welcome to get up on your own, but if you want a hand from the house band we're more than happy to fill in.
